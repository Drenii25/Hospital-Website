Welcome to Elite Care

Our Mission

At Sunrise Health Medical Center, our mission is simple yet powerful: to heal, to teach, to discover, and to advance the health of the communities we serve. We are dedicated to providing our patients with the highest quality of care in a respectful and dignified manner. Our focus is on innovation, collaboration, and empathy, ensuring that every patient receives the personalized care they deserve.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We provide care with kindness and empathy.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in everything we do.
  • Integrity: We conduct ourselves with honesty and fairness.
  • Collaboration: We work together to achieve common goals.
  • Innovation: We embrace change and seek better ways to serve our patients.

Our History

Founded in 1990 by a group of visionary healthcare professionals, Sunrise Health Medical Center has grown from a small community hospital to a comprehensive medical facility serving thousands of patients annually. Our history is marked by continuous improvement and expansion to meet the evolving health needs of our community.
